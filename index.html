Welcome to Windermere Gardens In-Home Care.

Welcome to Windermere Gardens In – Home Care. As a premiere in-home care provider, we offer exceptional non – medical services for adults, elderly and pediatric clients in the comfort of their own homes. It is in their own homes where our loved ones maintain their dignity and strength and feel the greatest sense of purpose. Our staff takes enormous pride in providing innovative and personalized services to our beloved senior citizen population.

Windermere Gardens provides hourly, overnight and 24 hour care that is tailored to the individual needs of the client. Whether at home, in an assisted living facility, nursing home, or hospital, we will provide care wherever care is needed.

Please contact us and let us know how Windermere Gardens can be of service to you by calling (678) 878-3205
